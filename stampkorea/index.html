KOREA GYUNGSANSNAM-DO

JEONJU HANOK VILLAGE

It is a representative travel destination of Korea, which is a collection of important cultural assets such as Gyeonggijeon Hall, Omokdae, Hyanggyo, and 20 cultural facilities, including Hanok, Korean food, Hanji, Hansori, Hanbok, and Hanbang.

Status of Hanok Village

Location: Pungnam-dong and Gyodong, Wansan-gu, Jeonju

Building: 947 Building (Hanok 735, Non-Hanok 212)

Number of businesses: 575 locations (stay 203, food and beverage 183 and other sales and rental 189)

  • Jeonju Hanok Village

    Craft Exhibition Hall

    • Exhibition (Tuesday~Sunday, 10:00~18:00)
    • - A permanent exhibition of more than 800 kinds of crafts in the Craft Exhibition Hall
    • - Exhibition of craftsmen's works on a variety of themes every week.
    • Experience (Free/Pay, 10:00-18:00)
    • - Experience programs that anyone can participate in, such as lovers and family members
    • - Traditional crafts such as fans, hanji crafts, and porcelain crafts
    • - Special experience for seasons (spring/summer/fall/winter), and seasons (Seollal/Chuseok)
    • - Free promotion of various traditional crafts and play experiences
